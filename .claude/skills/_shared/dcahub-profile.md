## About
DC&A Hub is a premium **research, data collection, and Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E)**
firm based in Accra, Ghana, serving local and international organisations across the
full research cycle — design, methodology, data collection, processing, analysis,
reporting, and visualization. Contact: info@dcahub.com · www.dcahub.com ·
P.O. Box CT 10685, Cantonments-Accra, Ghana.

## Services (what we do)
- **Research Design and Execution** — end-to-end research, from methodology development to reporting.
- **Data Collection, Analysis & Reporting** — field data collection (quantitative & qualitative), data processing, statistical analysis, reporting & visualization; enumerator recruitment & training; online/mobile survey programming.
- **Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)** — MEL framework design, results/performance frameworks, performance evaluations, baseline/midline/endline surveys, impact evaluation & assessment, learning reviews.
- **Third-Party Monitoring (TPM) & Data Quality Assessments (DQA)**.
- **Research & Consultancy Support** — literature reviews, stakeholder mapping, policy briefs, capacity building, KAP surveys, needs assessments, market studies, rapid assessments.
- **M&E systems** — design/development of web-based M&E systems (e.g. GIPC, World Bank).

## Sectors
Local governance & decentralisation; civil society strengthening; WASH / water &

agribusiness & youth livelihoods; digital / ICT / MSMEs; trade & economic policy;
gender equality & social inclusion (GESI); cocoa & living income; early childhood
development & education; employment / school-to-work; metrology & standards; waste
management & circular economy.

## Methodologies
Mixed methods (qualitative & quantitative); baseline/midline/endline surveys; impact
evaluation & assessment; KAP surveys; FGDs, KIIs, observations, structured
questionnaires; MEL/MEAL framework design; results frameworks & logframes; learning
reviews; GESI-responsive research; mobile/online data collection.

## Geographic experience
**Primary: Ghana** — operational resources and trained field agents (statisticians,
research assistants) in **all 16 regions** (Accra, Kumasi, Tamale, Takoradi, Ho,
Ashaiman, Sunyani, and beyond). **West Africa** experience via regional programmes
(e.g. Solidaridad West Africa). International research clients served on Ghana-based
assignments (UK, USA, EU institutions).
