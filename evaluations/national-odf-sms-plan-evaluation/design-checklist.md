## C. Open `[ASSUMPTION]` markers

1. **Framing / type.** Design inferred as a mixed-methods Mid-Term Evaluation OF the National ODF & SMS Plan, because no dedicated evaluation ToR exists — only the UNICEF framework-development ToR (which itself calls for outcome/impact evaluation, JSRs, and Mid-/End-Term Reviews). (Section: status note, 1.4)
2. **Evaluation type as formative MTE.** Treated as primarily formative with summative elements. (Section 1.4)

## D. Open `[REVIEW]` markers

Grouped by section.

**Understanding (Section 1)**
- Relative weight of accountability vs learning (1.2)
- Exact Plan start date & MTE cut-off / time period (1.4)
- Excluded geographies/periods/components (1.4)

**Evaluation questions (Section 2)**
- All EQ1–EQ7 and sub-questions flagged `[REVIEW]` — inferred from the framework ToR, not a dedicated evaluation ToR; to be confirmed/validated at inception.

**Sampling (Section 5)**
- Number of regions (4–6) `[REVIEW]`
- MMDAs per region (2–3) `[REVIEW]`
- Communities per MMDA (2–4) `[REVIEW]`
- Number of KIIs (40–60) `[REVIEW]`
- Number of FGDs (24–36) `[REVIEW]`
- Household survey sample size / precision / design effect — frame unknown `[REVIEW]`
- Disaggregation dimensions vs BaSIS/GSS variables (5.3) `[REVIEW]`

**Ethics & safeguarding (Section 8)**
- Scope of any direct engagement with children & alignment to UNICEF safeguarding standards `[REVIEW]`
- Whether institutional/national ethics-board approval is required `[REVIEW]`

**Limitations (Section 9)**
- Use of remote KIIs for access-constrained districts `[REVIEW]`

## E. Anti-fabrication confirmation
